About Me

Hi folks, I am Muhammed Raees.I am tech enthusiast, I like to learn and work in tech field especially in IT field. Right now I am doing an internship in a company called Brototype, to bacome a software engineer. Actually I am learning mobile app development using flutter. I am living in a small Village at Calicut in Kerala. I completed +2 in computer science in 2022, my studied till tenth standard was in local Schools in my village.

I am an introvert ,so usually I don't talk for no reason. I only talk about things I am interested in and about career too. talking and watching informative videos are the ways I used to gain knowledge. I like to gain knowledge about different things , especially about technology, science, business, politics . As an introvert I don't have friends its not only because of it, but also I never maintain friendship. Mostly I like be lonely, If I am alone I can think whatever I want and thinking is one of the another way that I gain knowledge and write. I also write my thoughts and share it with my friends in social media. I also like to write stories because I have a lots of stories in my mind.
I am also interested in entrepreneurship and business, I already made a try but it was a failure but defeinitely in future I will be an entrepreneur.
